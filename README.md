

Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer churn to identify factors that influence customer retention and customer loss. The goal is to help businesses understand why customers leave and provide data-driven recommendations to improve retention and revenue.

Objectives

Analyze customer churn patterns

Identify high-risk customer segments

Measure key churn-related KPIs

Create an interactive Power BI dashboard

Generate actionable business insights

Key Business Insights

Customers with month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate.

Fiber internet users are more likely to leave the service.

Customers using electronic check payment methods show higher churn.

Customers with longer tenure contribute more revenue.

Early-stage customers are at greater risk of churn.


Business Recommendations

Offer incentives for long-term contracts.

Improve customer support for high-risk segments.

Introduce personalized retention campaigns.

Monitor customer complaints and support tickets proactively.

Enhance customer engagement during the first few months.
